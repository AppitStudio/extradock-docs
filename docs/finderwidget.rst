Finder Widget
=============

The Finder widget opens Finder from your dock.

It is useful when you want Finder access in a dock replacement setup, or when you want a dock that opens a specific folder.

Location
--------

Choose where Finder opens:

- **Home**
- **Documents**
- **Downloads**
- **Desktop**
- **Applications**
- **Custom**

Use **Custom** to choose any folder on your Mac.

Behavior
--------

Choose what happens when you click the widget:

- **Open New Window** — Open Finder at the selected location
- **Activate Finder** — Bring Finder forward

Running indicator
-----------------

The Finder widget can show a running indicator dot when Finder is active.
