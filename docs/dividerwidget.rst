Divider Widget
==============

The Divider widget adds a visible separator line between dock items.

Use dividers to group related apps, separate widgets from apps, or make a full dock easier to scan.

Adding a divider
----------------

1. Open the management window → select a dock → **Items**
2. Click **Add Widget**
3. Choose **Divider**

Appearance
----------

Open the divider's settings to change:

- **Line color** — The divider color
- **Side spacing** — Clear space on both sides of the line

The divider adapts automatically to horizontal and vertical docks.
