Clock Widget
============

The Clock widget shows a live clock in your dock.

It can be simple and minimal, or show date, timezone, theme, and custom styling.

Format and layout
-----------------

Choose:

- **12-Hour** or **24-Hour** time
- **Show Seconds**
- **Show Date**
- **Show Timezone**
- Layout presets such as **Minimal**, **Standard**, **Detailed**, **Full**, or **Custom**

Date and timezone
-----------------

The clock can use your system timezone or a selected timezone such as New York, Los Angeles, London, Paris, Tokyo, Sydney, or UTC.

For date display, choose no date, short date, long date, or a custom format.

Appearance
----------

Customize:

- Font family
- Font weight
- Theme
- Corner radius
- Text color
- Background color
- Border color
- Shadow intensity

Themes include options such as Classic, Monochrome, Neon, Minimalist, Professional, Retro, and Liquid Glass.

Click action
------------

Choose what happens when you click the clock:

- **No Action**
- **Open System Preferences**
- **Open Clock Settings**
